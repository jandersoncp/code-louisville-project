PRIVATE DINING

Premier At Home

Imagine enjoying the best restaurant in town, right in your own dining room. Our Chefs personalize each menu to cater to you, creating an unforgettable dining experience for your family, friends or significant other.

Add a handcrafted cocktail, sommelier pairing or brew to enhance your meal.

COOKING CLASSES

Sushi Night

Make your own signature Sushi Roll like a pro! In this class guests will create nigiri, sashimi and their own signature rolls. Guests will have an array of seafood and fresh produce and learn to make sushi rice, prepare ingredients, and the traditional rolling techniques to ensure a perfect roll every time.


Enhance this experience with a traditional Sake Tasting.

Pasta Making

Ever wanted to learn how to make homemade, fresh pasta? In this class you will spend a few hours learning to make fettuccine and raviolis from scratch. You will also learn to make pesto and Chianti Marinara. Finally, you will sit down to enjoy a traditional Italian Meal. All guests will have fresh pasta to take home and enjoy later.


Enhance this experience with a paired sommelier wine selection.

Pizza Class

On the hunt for a casual, yet fun meal to cook with friends? Premier's one-of-a-kind pizza class combines local ingredients, homemade sauce and fresh dough to create the perfect pizza. In this class, guests will roll dough, top their pies with a variety of tasty sauces and create a unique dish with their choice of fresh ingredients.

Enhance this experience with your choice of our handpicked brews.
